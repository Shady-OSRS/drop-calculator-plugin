Advanced Drop Calculator

A robust and intuitive drop calculator plugin for Old School RuneScape, designed to help you track your RNG and expected drops.

## Features
* **Probability Check:** Calculates the exact chance of receiving at least one drop based on your current KC.
* **Fraction Support:** Fully supports fraction drop rates directly from the OSRS Wiki (e.g., `3/408`) or standard denominators (e.g., `1/300` or `300`).
* **Expected Drops:** Compares your KC to the drop rate to show how many drops you *should* theoretically have.
* **Dry Limits:** Displays the KC required to hit a 50% coin-flip chance and the 99% Dry Limit threshold.
